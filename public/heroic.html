HEROIC stun PARIVISION to reach EPL Stage 2

The PGL Cluj-Napoca runners-up have been eliminated after suffering three straight losses.

HEROIC have locked up a spot in Stage 2 of ESL Pro League 23 after a dominant 2-0 victory against PARIVISION in the 2-2 pool.

It's a disappointing elimination for the current world No.5, who were unable to convert a 2-0 record into a Stage 2 berth. After losing progression series to Legacy and Monte, they turned in a horribly beige performance against a HEROIC team still using a makeshift AWPer in Simon "⁠yxngstxr⁠" Boije.

The defeat is likely to sting, but PARIVISION can certainly chalk it up to fatigue after a taxing run at PGL Cluj-Napoca, where they had to play 23 maps across eight series.

Dzhami "⁠Jame⁠" Ali's troops will have two weeks to rest and recover before their next tournament, BLAST Open Rotterdam, where they will face Ninjas in Pyjamas in their opening match-up.

HEROIC came into the series full of impetus and stormed to a 7-5 lead on the T side of Inferno. After picking up a four-round streak towards the end of the first half, PARIVISION were expected to carry that momentum into their T side, but instead it was all HEROIC, who went on a dominant 6-0 run, finishing the game with an impressive 15-3 opening kill record.

Yasin "⁠xfl0ud⁠" Koç took over the game on Dust2, posting a 17-4 K-D as HEROIC once again took a 7-5 lead. One round was all that PARIVISION could muster on the CT side as HEROIC didn't waste the opportunity to kill the game off.
