makazze claims career-first MVP at EPL S23 finals

A first for makazze and a first for Kosovo as the 19-year-old pips w0nderful to the highest individual accolade in Sweden.

Drin "⁠makazze⁠" Shaqiri has been named the HLTV x 1xBet Most Valuable Player of ESL Pro League Season 23 Finals after leading Natus Vincere's title-winning efforts in Sweden.

It is the Kosovar's career-first MVP award, and he becomes the first player from his country to earn the accolade in the process.

makazze did so thanks to a consistent showing all throughout the Stockholm finals, which he concluded with all nine maps he played in the LAN portion rated above 1.00.

He began the finals with a demolition of The MongolZ, went on to star on the Dust2 decider against FUT, and was only pipped by Ihor "⁠w0nderful⁠" Zhdanov in a superb grand final performance against Aurora overall.

That amounted to makazze recording some chart-topping numbers throughout the tournament: a 1.38 rating (joint-first), 92.3 ADR (first), 0.83 kills per round (joint-first), 80.5% KAST (first) and +4.10% Round Swing (second).

After making a compelling case of his own with an unmatched grand final performance, makazze's Ukrainian teammate is sure to end up on top of the list of Exceptionally Valuable Players.
